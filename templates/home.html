HOW IT WORKS...
Step 1
Enter product name
The user types in the name or description of an AI-powered toy, robot, or digital assistant made for kids.

Step 2
AI Analysis Begins
KoAi uses a powerful language model (like GPT) to analyze the emotional tone and ethical implications of the productâ€™s behavior, voice, and messaging.

Step 3
Emotional & Ethical Scanning
The system checks for signs of emotional manipulation, inappropriate influence, or ethical red flags in how the product interacts with children.

Step 3
Safety Scoring
KoAi generates a visual safety score based on friendliness, emotional tone, and ethical trustworthiness.

Step 4
Verdict & Suggestions
The user gets a simple result with a verdict (Safe / Caution / Unsafe), plus helpful suggestions for safer alternatives or improvements.
